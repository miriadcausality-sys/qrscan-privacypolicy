Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

miriad.causality ("Developer," "I," "me," or "my") operates the Qr & Barcode Scanner mobile application (the "Service").

Your privacy is extremely important to me. Because this application is built to run entirely offline and respects your privacy, I do not collect, store, use, or share any of your personal data.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand the practices regarding your information.

1. Information Collection and Use

  • No Personal Data Collection: I do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, phone number, physical address, or precise location.
  • No Offline Tracking: The App operates completely locally on your device. I do not track your behavioral data, app usage patterns, or device identifiers.
  • No Third-Party Analytics: I do not use any third-party analytics software (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) to monitor the performance or usage of the App.

2. Device Permissions

The App may require certain device permissions to function correctly (for example, camera access to scan codes or storage access to save history locally). These permissions are used strictly for the core functionality of the App on your device. No data accessed through these permissions is transmitted outside of your device.

3. Advertisements

This App does not contain any advertisements, nor do I partner with any ad networks. Therefore, no advertising identifiers are collected or shared.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not connect to any external servers, APIs, or third-party services. Your data never leaves your mobile device.

5. Children's Privacy

Because this App does not collect any personal information from any user, it does not collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. This App is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and global privacy regulations regarding minors.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App is offline and I cannot notify you directly, any updates will be made available by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page or updating the policy within the Google Play Store listing. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact me.
